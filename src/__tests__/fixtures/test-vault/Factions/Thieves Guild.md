# Thieves Guild

**Type:** Criminal faction
**Base of operations:** [[Port Calderon]]
**Leader:** [[Mira Shadowcloak]]
**Status:** Active

## Goals
Control the black market trade routes through the Shattered Coast.
Maintain plausible deniability with the city watch.

## Allies
- [[Harbor Master Voss]] — paid informant
- [[The Pale Merchant]] — fence for stolen goods

## Enemies
- [[Inquisitors of the Coin]] — mercantile enforcement order
- [[Dock Workers Union]] — competing for harbor control

## Notes
The Guild does not tolerate killing civilians. It is a business, not
a war band. Members who draw unnecessary attention are dealt with
quietly.
