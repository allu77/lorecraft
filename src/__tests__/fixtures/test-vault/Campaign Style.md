# Campaign Style — The Shattered Coast

%% == LORECRAFT: CAMPAIGN STYLE == %%

## Setting
A dark, coastal fantasy world. City-states compete for trade routes.
Magic is rare and distrusted by common folk.

## Tone
Gritty and grounded. Think Joe Abercrombie, not Tolkien. Moral
ambiguity is expected. Heroes have flaws; villains have reasons.

## Hard rules
- No resurrection magic exists in this world
- Gods are distant and do not intervene directly
- Never invent a character's age — leave the Age field blank if unknown

## Inspirations
- The First Law trilogy (Joe Abercrombie)
- Lies of Locke Lamora (Scott Lynch)
