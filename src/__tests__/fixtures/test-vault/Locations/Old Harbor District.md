# Old Harbor District

**Location:** [[Port Calderon]]
**Status:** Contested

## Overview
The oldest part of the city, where dockworkers load and unload cargo under the
watchful eye of [[Harbor Master Voss]]. The warehouses here change hands often.

## Notable Features
- The Tar & Tallow — a tavern where dockworkers drink after shift
- Bonded Warehouse Row — long-term storage, controlled by the [[Thieves Guild]]
- The Rat's Nest — a cramped tenement above a chandler's shop

## Dangers
Smugglers use the network of sea-caves beneath the district to move contraband.
The city watch patrols only the main thoroughfare; the back alleys belong to
whoever is boldest after dark.

## Connections
- [[Harbor Master Voss]] oversees legitimate trade
- [[Thieves Guild]] taxes the smugglers through informal arrangement
