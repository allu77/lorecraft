# Mira Shadowcloak

**Role:** Guild Master, [[Thieves Guild]]
**Location:** [[Port Calderon]]
**Age:**
**Status:** Alive

## Description
A lean woman with close-cropped grey hair and the unhurried manner of
someone who has never needed to raise her voice. She communicates
displeasure through silence.

## Personality
Pragmatic to the point of coldness. Loyal to profit, not people.
Respects competence above all else.

## Secret
She was once an Inquisitor of the Coin. She knows their methods better
than they know themselves.

## Connections
- Leader of [[Thieves Guild]]
- Old enemy: [[Commander Aldric]] (former colleague)
